• Elbow Knee escape
    Link
    Held from your right. Your right knee against opponents hip
    to stop them mounting. Get your right hand in between their
    arm and knee. Can turn to the side to get it in.
    Use your left foot on the floor to shrimp away
    and gradually bring your right knee in to touch your elbow.
    Then put left foot on your right foot and turn to be face on
    to your opponent. Get inside hand on the hand which is controlling
    your head.
    Secret to getting out of pins is to move from
    perpendicular spines to having spines aligned.
  • Escape against heavier opponent:
    Opponent has you in side control to your right. They switch the base to
    Kesa Gatame legs. Your left hand grabs their same side lapel
    to prevent your hand getting attacked. Bridge to the right and stretch out your arms, then bring your right knee in.
    Bridge to the right creates the space.
